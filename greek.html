Baklava

prep time

35 min.

cook time

30 min.

Servings

18

instructions

step 1

Set oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C) to preheat.

step 2

In a large bowl, mix together the chopped nuts, sugar, and cinnamon.

step 3

Brush a 9x13 inch baking dish with some of the melted butter.

step 4

Lay one sheet of phyllo dough in the dish, and brush it with butter. Repeat with 10 more sheets of phyllo, brushing each one with butter before adding the next.

step 5

cover the phyllo with half the nut mixture. Repeat the process of layering with 10 sheets of phyllo and the remaining nut mixture. Finish with a top layer of 10 to 12 sheets of phyllo, brushing each one with butter before adding the next.

step 6

cut up the baklava into squares or diamonds Utilizing a sharp knife,

step 7

In a small saucepan, combine and heat honey, water, and lemon juice utlizing low heat and stir until well blended.

step 8

Pour the syrup over the baklava

step 9

Bake in the preheated oven until the top is golden brown.

step 10

Allow to cool before serving.

ingredients

1 lb phyllo dough

1 pound chopped nuts

1 lb unsalted butter, melted

1 tsp ground cinnamon

1/2 cup water

1 cup sugar

1/2 cup honey

1 tbsp lemon juice

Greek Meatballls

prep time

20-25 min.

cook time

30-35 min

servings

4

instructions

step 1

In a large bowl, combine the ground meat, breadcrumbs, milk, egg, onion, garlic, parsley, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.

step 2

Shape the mixture into 1 1/2-inch meatballs.

step 3

Heat the olive oil over medium to high heat in a skillet. Add the meatballs and cook until browned on all over.

step 4

Reduce the heat to medium to low and add the tomato sauce and broth.

step 5

Simmer the meatballs in the sauce for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until they are cooked through.

step 6

Serve the meatballs over spaghetti or with crusty bread to soak up the sauce.

ingredients

1/2 cup milk

1/2 cup chopped onion

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 pound ground beef or lamb

1 tsp dried oregano

1/2 cup bread crumbs

1 tsp dried thyme

1 tsp salt

1/2 tsp black pepper

1 tbsp olive oil

1 egg

2 cups tomato sauce

1 cup beef or chicken broth

Greek Yogurt Cheesecake

prep time

20-25 min.

cook time

35-40 mins

servings

10 servings

instructions

step 1

Preheat oven to 350Â°F and grease a 9-inch springform pan.

step 2

In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, 1/4 cup of the sugar, and vanilla extract. Pour this mixture into a large pan.

step 3

Put cream cheese in a large bowl and beat it until it is smooth. Add the remaining 1/2 cup sugar, Greek yogurt, eggs, flour, and lemon juice and mix until well combined.

step 4

Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the prepared pan.

step 5

Bake the cheesecake for 35-40 minutes, or until the center is almost set.

step 6

Let the cheesecake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then remove the sides of the pan and let the cheesecake cool completely.

step 7

Chill the cheesecake in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving.

step 8

Stir together blueberries, brown sugar, poppy seeds, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Scatter topping over cheesecake.

ingredients

For Crust:

6 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs

3/4 cup sugar

For Filling:

4 large eggs

1/4 cup all-purpose flour

4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, at room temperature

1 1/4 cups plain Greek yogurt

1/4 cup all-purpose flour

2 tbsp lemon juice

1 tbsp vanilla extract

For Topping:

4 cups fresh blueberries

3 tablespoons packed dark brown sugar

2 tablespoons lemon juice

1 teaspoon lemon zest

Greek Yogurt Pancakes

prep time

10-15 min.

cook time

10-15 min

Servings

4

instructions

step 1

Put the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl and beat these ingredients together.

step 2

In another large bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, milk, eggs, vanilla extract, and melted butter.

step 3

Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, whisking until just combined. Do not overmix.

step 4

Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat and grease with oil or butter.

step 5

Using a 1/4 cup measure, pour batter onto the pan for each pancake.

step 6

Cook the pancakes in large pan until both sides are golden brown.

step 7

Repeat steps 1-6 with any batter remaining.

step 8

Serve the pancakes hot with your desired toppings, such as syrup, fresh fruit, or whipped cream.

ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour

1/4 tsp baking soda

2 tsp baking powder

1/4 tsp salt

2 tbsp sugar

1/2 cup milk

2 large eggs

1 tsp vanilla extract

1 cup plain Greek yogurt

1 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

Oil or butter, for greasing the pan

Gyros

prep time

15 min.

cook time

15 min

Servings

4

instructions

step 1

In a large bowl, combine the ground lamb or beef, minced onion, garlic, oregano, thyme, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.

step 2

Divide the mixture into 4 portions and shape each portion into an oblong patty.

step 3

Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and cook the patties for 5-7 minutes on each side, or until browned and cooked through.

step 4

Toast the pita breads in the oven or on a dry skillet until warm and slightly crispy.

step 5

Spread a generous amount of tzatziki sauce on each pita bread.

step 6

Place a patty in each pita bread and top with diced tomatoes, diced onion, and sliced cucumber.

step 7

Roll the pita breads around the filling and serve immediately.

ingredients

1/2 cup minced onion

3 cloves garlic, minced

2 tbsp dried oregano

2 tbsp dried thyme

2 tsp ground cumin

1 tsp paprika

1 pound lamb or beef

Salt and pepper, to taste

4 pita breads

Tzatziki sauce, diced tomatoes, diced onion, and sliced cucumber, for serving
